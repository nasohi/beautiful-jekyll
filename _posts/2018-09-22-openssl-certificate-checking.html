--- layout: post title: OpenSSL certificate checking date: '2018-09-22T22:24:00.003+07:00' author: Nasohi Ciptandani tags: - ITNSA - SSL modified_time: '2018-09-22T22:26:49.583+07:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-7821506235811301823.post-8900600312773121843 blogger_orig_url: https://nciptandani.blogspot.com/2018/09/openssl-certificate-checking.html --- Check a certificate
Information about it (signing authority, expiration date, etc.):

   openssl x509 -in server.crt -text -noout

Check a key
Check the SSL key and verify the consistency:

   openssl rsa -in server.key -check

Check a CSR
Verify the CSR and print CSR data filled in when generating the CSR:

   openssl req -text -noout -verify -in server.csr

Verify a certificate and key matches
The checksums can be compared to verify that the certificate and key match.

   openssl x509 -noout -modulus -in server.crt | openssl md5
   openssl rsa -noout -modulus -in server.key | openssl md5

Verifying that a Certificate is issued by a CA
Verify that a certificate was issued by a specific CA, given that CA's certificate

   openssl verify -verbose -CAfile cacert.pem server.crt
