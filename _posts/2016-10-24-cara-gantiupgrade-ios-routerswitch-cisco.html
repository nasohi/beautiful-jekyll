--- layout: post title: Cara ganti/upgrade ios router/switch cisco date: '2016-10-24T15:30:00.002+07:00' author: Nasohi Ciptandani tags: - Cisco modified_time: '2016-11-21T10:20:26.974+07:00' thumbnail: https://1.bp.blogspot.com/-WPB4ZvHIVxo/WDJn47LK-mI/AAAAAAAAA-8/MH_Nj_vK3yQK51fo7A_AF4NhU1FvzzAGQCLcB/s72-c/ccnav5-s3-chapter9-ios-images-and-licensing-13-638.jpg blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-7821506235811301823.post-5890366498241316045 blogger_orig_url: https://nciptandani.blogspot.com/2016/10/cara-gantiupgrade-ios-routerswitch-cisco.html --- Kali ini saya upgrade ios untuk router, saya pakai flashdisk, ios yang baru saya taruh didalam flashdisk, dan colokkan flashdisk ke router, semoga router anda ada colokkan usbnya.


Jika kita sedang on di terminal maka akan muncul %USBFLASH-5-CHANGE: usbflash0 has been inserted!  saat kita mencolokkan flahdisk, itu informational saja


Cek isi flahdisk
#dir usbflash0:

Copy/backup ios yang lama di direktori flash:(nama disk penyimpanan di  router cisco)  ke usbflash0/
RouterABC1#copy flash:/c2900-universalk9-mz.SPA.154-3.M4.bin usbflash0:/
Destination filename [c2900-universalk9-mz.SPA.154-3.M4.bin]?


Kalau mau delete ios yang lama
RouterABC1#delete flash:/c2900-universalk9-mz.SPA.154-3.M4.bin


Copy/backup ios yang baru dari usbflash ke flash:
RouterABC1#copy usbflash0:/c2900-universalk9-mz.SPA.154-3.M5.bin flash0:/
Destination filename [c2900-universalk9-mz.SPA.154-3.M5.bin]?

Cek isi flash0:
#dir flash0:

Optional untuk verifikasi file apakah corrupt atau tidak
RouterABC1#verify flash:c2900-universalk9-mz.SPA.154-3.M5.bin
Setting bootnya ke yang ios baru 

RouterABC1#boot system flash:/c2900-universalk9-mz.SPA.154-3.M5.bin
Simpan #wr mem dan #reload
